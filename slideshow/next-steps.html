After you ship — your punch list

Four things to do on launch day.

You've got a site. Now set up the compounding wins — the stuff that multiplies your traffic for zero extra content.
1
Mandatory · local
Google Business Profile
Claim your local listing
If you're a local service business, this is non-negotiable. Shows up in Google Maps + the local 3-pack — massive free traffic.
Do this Claim. Verify address. Fill every field. Ask customers for reviews.
2
Free
Google Search Console
Track impressions & rankings
Google tells you exactly which keywords you rank for, how often you're shown, and what gets clicked.
Do this Verify domain. Submit sitemap.xml. Request indexing on key pages.
3
Free
Google Analytics (GA4)
Track visitors & behaviour
See which pages get traffic, where visitors come from, how long they stay, and what they do next.
Do this Install the GA4 snippet. Set conversion events (form submit, phone tap).
4
Ongoing
CRO — split testing
Test your landing pages
Same traffic, higher conversion = more leads. Test one thing at a time — headline, CTA, hero image — find the winner, run with it.
Do this Start with the hero headline. Swap quarterly. Keep the winner.
!
Don't do this
Don't dump 1,000 blog posts on day one.
Google sees the spam pattern and deprioritises your whole site. Drip them out. Slow and steady wins this game.
100/day → 2–5/week
Ship it → set up these four → drip content weekly. In 3 months you'll have real data. In 12, real traffic.

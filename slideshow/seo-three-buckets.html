Before we get into the weeds…

SEO is 3 buckets.

Get all three right and you rank. Skip one and you don't. That's it.
100% in your control
Bucket 1
On-page SEO
Everything on your page — the stuff you write, structure, and publish.
Title tags Headings Content Keywords Internal links Images & alt text Schema
Mostly automated
Bucket 2
Technical SEO
How your site is built and served — speed, crawlability, infrastructure.
Sitemap.xml Robots.txt Site speed Core Web Vitals Mobile-friendly HTTPS Canonicals
Slowest, hardest
Bucket 3
Off-page SEO
What the rest of the internet says about you — earned, not built.
Backlinks Google reviews Brand mentions Local citations Social signals PR Referring domains
Bucket 1 + 2 = Claude Code handles 95%. Bucket 3 = your reputation, earned over time.
